APP Features
Privacy

Carme also provides you with the feature of broadcasting your requirements at one click. So now no need to contact the donors individually, all you need to do is just search your requirements by selecting blood group and distance, and click broadcast button and all the donors will receive the message with your contact details and will contact you directly.

Pollution Reminder

Forgetting the pollution control papers’ renewal is no more a problem, since it is looked after by Carme now! Car owners can just enter their pollution details, and the next renewal prompt is given by Carme.

Insurence Reminder

Similarly, car insurance would not be missed anymore. Users can put in the insurance details and the renewal aide-mémoire is Carme’s obligation thereafter.

Picture Upload

What more to ensure the validation of the car identity than a picture itself, for Carme lets the users add the car’s pictures as well as the owners’ pictures to the application, thereby giving no chance of mismanagement.

More Features One User Multiple Vehicles

One user can add multiple vehicles, however, one vehicle can be entered by only one user, to avoid multiple entries and chaos thereafter!

One Vehicle Multiple Members

One user can add multiple members to a single vehicle, wherein the members belong to same family and can be available to move the vehicle, when required.

No Duplicate Entries

A vehicle once entered, cannot be added by another user without the initial owner’s permission. In case of second hand buys, the first owner needs to delete the vehicle details from his/her profile, to let the second owner enter the vehicle in his/her name.

Easy To Notify

The trapped user can notify the other vehicle’s owner via call, SMS, or an alert in the application, which would intimate the vehicle owner to move their vehicle from the parking in order to let the trapped user move their vehicle.
